::: {.callout-note appearance="simple"}
This course runs entirely on the [replication datasets](../datasets/index.md) we ship for you —
you won't need an external corpus for any tutorial, Corpus Lab, or the final mini-project. The
links below are **optional**, for anyone who wants to explore language data beyond the course
(e.g. follow-on research). They are provided as-is; check each source's own license before use.
:::

## English / multilingual corpus tools

- [CorpusMate](https://corpusmate.com/info)
- [Montclair State University — CORAL lab](https://www.coralcorpuslab.com)

## Japanese corpora

- [NINJAL-LWP for TWC](https://tsukubawebcorpus.jp/)
- [名大会話コーパス (Nagoya University Conversation Corpus)](https://mmsrv.ninjal.ac.jp/nucc/)
- [日本語対話コーパス一覧 (Japanese dialogue corpus list)](https://individuality.jp/dialogue_corpus.html)
- [関西弁コーパス (Kansai dialect corpus)](https://sites.google.com/view/kvjcorpus/%E3%83%9B%E3%83%BC%E3%83%A0?authuser=0)

- [現代日本語書き言葉均衡コーパス (BCCWJ) 公開データ](https://clrd.ninjal.ac.jp/bccwj/bcc-chu.html)
- [日本語話し言葉コーパス (CSJ)](https://clrd.ninjal.ac.jp/csj/chunagon.html)
- [国語研日本語ウェブコーパス](https://www.gsk.or.jp/catalog/gsk2020-d/)
- [Wortschatz Japanese corpus (Leipzig)](https://wortschatz.uni-leipzig.de/en/download/Japanese)
